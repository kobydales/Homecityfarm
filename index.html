Our story

Quality and integrity

For as long as we can remember, our founder has always had a passion for animals and the land. After many years of dedicated professional work, he chose to pursue that lifelong dream by establishing HomeCity Farms.

What began as a personal passion has grown into a family-run farm built on honesty, integrity, and quality. We believe that if something is worth doing, it is worth doing well. Every animal on our farm is raised with care, and every product we offer reflects the standards we set for ourselves. We never cut corners because our name—and our promise to our customers—stands behind everything we produce.
